About Me

Building the Future with AI & Code

I'm a passionate developer from Kathmandu, Nepal, currently studying at Coventry University. My expertise spans AI development, full-stack engineering, and game development. I specialize in building real-time applications, implementing computer vision systems, and creating scalable web solutions.

From developing Nepal's first real-time task marketplace with live GPS tracking to creating immersive 3D games with Unity, I love tackling complex challenges. My work includes production-ready applications deployed on platforms like Vercel, comprehensive school management systems, and innovative QR-based exhibition systems with Nepali text-to-speech integration.

Philosophy: "Jack of all trades, master of none, but oftentimes better than master of one."
